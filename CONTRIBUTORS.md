## Yassen Eltayeb — lead author

Conefia LLC · [dev@conefia.com](mailto:dev@conefia.com)

**Conceptualization** · **Methodology** · **Investigation** · **Supervision** ·
**Project administration** · **Writing — original draft**

Conceived SAFE-CARE Bench as the evaluation instrument for the *Evaluate* principle of the
[SAFE-CARE framework](https://github.com/Conefia/SAFE-CARE), of which he is the sole author.
Designed the A0–A4 ablation ladder and its capability-bundle structure, the eight scoring dimensions
and their anchor definitions, the separation of single-turn from multi-turn safety and escalation
evaluation, and the hard-fail override on missed escalation. Authored the judge prompts, the
scenario schema and scenario set, the persona methodology, the public-release boundary, and the
repository documentation. Directs the technical programme.

## Contributing

External contributions are welcome and will be acknowledged here. The most valuable contributions
right now are judge calibration data, additional red-flag and over-escalation scenarios, and persona
expansion. See [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](CONTRIBUTING.md).

Contributors retain copyright in their own contributions and license them to the project under the
repository's stated licences. Contribution does not transfer ownership of Conefia IP, and does not
create an ownership interest in the benchmark as a whole.
